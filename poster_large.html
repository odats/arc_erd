Method

Analogical reasoning = estimating a shared translation x̂.

Per-pair differences
δi = mean(bi − ai)
Closed-form least-squares estimate
x̂ = (1/n) Σi δi
Apply and train
L = CE(decode(ai + x̂), bi)
*No auxiliary latent losses
